Everything You Need to Drive Engagement

Instant Emoji Reactions Pro packs advanced customization, analytics and integrations into one lightweight, performance-optimized plugin.

Custom Reaction Database

A dedicated, indexed reaction table stores post ID, emoji ID, user/guest identity, IP hash and timestamp — built for performance, reporting and clean migrations.

Gutenberg Block

Drop the Emoji Reactions block into any post or page, then control layout, totals and visibility directly from the Block Editor — no shortcode required.

Automatic Placement

Show reactions before or after content on posts, pages, WooCommerce products and public custom post types. Shortcode and block placement always take priority when present.

Custom Emoji Sets

Pick up to 12 emojis from a searchable catalog, apply quick presets (Facebook-style, Minimal, Like/Love/Laugh), reorder them, or override the set per post or shortcode.

Advanced Analytics Dashboard

Unlimited storage with site-wide metrics, engagement rate, per-post reports, emoji breakdowns, visual charts, date filters and top-content rankings.

CSV Export

Export site summaries, per-post reaction reports and raw reaction logs as CSV — perfect for deeper analysis outside WordPress.

WooCommerce, bbPress & BuddyPress

Add reactions to product pages and product reviews, forum topics and replies, and BuddyPress activity streams — each integration individually toggleable.

Reaction Toggle & Switching

One reaction per visitor per post. Click the same emoji to remove it, or click another to switch instantly — with loading spinners and disabled states while saving.

Social Proof Displays

Show live per-emoji counts, an optional "142 Total Reactions" line, a "John, Sarah and 12 others reacted" line, and a "Most Popular" badge.

Design & Live Preview

Horizontal, vertical or compact layouts with Facebook, Modern, Minimal and Slack style presets, custom colors, and a live preview before you save.

Spam Protection & Privacy

Nonce-secured AJAX, rate limiting, max reactions per IP, bot/crawler exclusion, guest deduplication, GDPR mode, data retention cleanup and optional clean uninstall.

Performance & Cache Safe

AJAX-powered vanilla JavaScript (no jQuery), optimized queries, and full compatibility with LiteSpeed Cache, WP Rocket and W3 Total Cache.
