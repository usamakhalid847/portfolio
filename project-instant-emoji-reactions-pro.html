Anti-Abuse & Privacy Controls

Rate limiting, per-IP guest caps, bot exclusion, login-only mode, GDPR mode (no IP storage), automatic data retention cleanup and an optional clean uninstall.

Auto-insert before or after content, use the Gutenberg block or shortcode, or override placement per post — manual placement always wins.
